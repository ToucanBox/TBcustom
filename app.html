Printing advice


We know it’s not always easy to get printing working on your phone, tablet and even your desktop! Here’s some advice to give you a helping hand:

  • If your device supports it, you can print to a digital PDF document. You can then email this document to yourself and print later.
  • Some printers even support printing email attachments directly, you just need to find the email address of the printer.
  • If you don’t have the option to print, or to save a PDF you can save an image. On a touch device, touch and hold the image until a menu appears (right click if using a mouse). Choose the option to save/download the image.
  • If none of the above work you can save the print preview image on the previous page. Even though the image looks small it’s the same as the full size image, just scaled down.
  • Any questions or troubleshooting (or praise) please email ant@toucanbox.com
